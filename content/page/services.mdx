I work with founders, small teams, and engineering leaders who want a thoughtful collaborator rather than just an extra pair of hands. Engagements range from a one-off discovery call to multi-month builds. I'm based in **Stockholm (CET)** and work async-first, so timezone overlap is rarely a blocker.

## Build

For when you need something designed, shipped, or rebuilt:

- **Web design & development** — Modern marketing sites, app frontends, and full rebuilds in **Next.js / TypeScript**, with care for performance, accessibility, and content workflows.
- **Product development** — From discovery through delivery. I'm comfortable owning a slice end-to-end or embedding into an existing team to move things forward.
- **AI strategy & implementation** — Practical AI integrations, prompt and eval workflows, and rolling out **AI-assisted development** so your team ships more, not less carefully.
- **Logo & brand design** — Marks, wordmarks, and lightweight identity systems for early-stage products that need a coherent face.

## Advise

For shorter, lower-touch engagements where the goal is clarity:

- **Personal career advice** — 1:1 sessions for engineers and engineering leaders navigating growth, scope changes, or transitions.
- **Marketing** — Positioning, messaging, and go-to-market guidance for technical products and indie founders.

## How we'd work together

Most engagements follow the same shape:

- A free **30-minute discovery call** to understand the problem and see if I'm the right fit.
- A short written **proposal** with scope, timeline, and pricing — fixed quote or hourly, whichever fits better.
- **Async-first** working style: shared docs, short async updates, and a weekly sync if useful.
- Billing **monthly in arrears** for hourly work, milestone-based for fixed-price projects.
